This application is a requirement for completing the Practical Exam at our school.
A movie review/database app utilizing TMDB API. User can search and view details for any movie/tv series.

If user has am TMDB account already, they can connect with their account and directly rate movies from the app. Further more it'll also unlock other abilities like able to see movies the account saved.
