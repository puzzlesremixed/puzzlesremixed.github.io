I made few WhatsApp bot for an experiment, using both [wweb-js](https://wwebjs.dev/) and [Baileys](https://baileys.wiki/).
In the beginning this was just a way for me to fun with my circle of friends, but later on this will prove useful when our Automatic Printing system on Rantai Media Digital needs a bot integration. That one was made with a collaboration with [Zahran Zaidan Nasution.](https://github.com/Aran8276)
